EmpowerMind Mobile Application

Last Updated: October 31, 2024

At EmpowerMind, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best learning experience. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile information (avatar, bio, preferences)
  • Login credentials (securely encrypted)
  • Language preferences

1.2 Usage Information

To improve your experience, we automatically collect:

  • Course progress and completion data
  • Lesson interaction statistics
  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • Crash reports and error logs

1.3 Information You Provide

  • Feedback and support requests
  • Reviews and ratings
  • User-generated content (notes, comments)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Enable account creation, course access, and progress tracking
  • Personalization: Customize content recommendations based on your learning history
  • Communication: Send important updates, notifications, and educational content
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication options available
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 We May Share Data With:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in app functionality (cloud hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app performance and usage
  • Provide personalized content

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In
  • Cloud Storage: Google Cloud Platform

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
